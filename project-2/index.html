Description

This Instructable will be on how to make Lego Key Holder with a note clip

Materials

  • Lego base plate
  • Lego bricks
  • Adhesive Putty
  • Spirit Level
  • Keys on a key ring

    • Directions

    1. Take a Lego base plate and stick adhesive putty or pads to the back of it. For a more permanent solution drill through the base plate and fix it to the wall with screws. 2. Fix the base plate to a suitable place on the wall. Use a spirit level to make sure its straight. 3. Thread a small Lego flat onto your keyring or chain. 4. Hang your keys on the board and decorate your Lego base plate with more Lego blocks. 5. Add a Lego hinge to use as a note holder.
